Welcome to Guess Game!

Rules of Guess game:

  1. Your goal is to guess Cities from choosen area.
  2. You need to search Cities for given area by typing City name in field.
  3. If you think you have all Cities names choosen, you will need to click button "See My Results" so you could see you score.
  4. Don't forget that you have 100 seconds to complete the game.
